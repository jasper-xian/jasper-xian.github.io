Hi! I'm a final-year undergraduate at the University of Waterloo studying computer science. Upon graduation, I'll be joining [Google DeepMind](https://deepmind.google/) as a research engineer on the [foundational research team](https://deepmind.google/research/).

My research interests generally lie at the intersection of information retrieval and LLMs. I'm incredibly fortunate to be advised by [Jimmy Lin](https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~jimmylin/) at Waterloo, and [Christopher Potts](https://stanford.edu/~cgpotts/) & [Omar Khattab](https://omarkhattab.com/) at Stanford.

I've also done internships at [Google DeepMind](https://deepmind.google/) and [Co:here](https://cohere.com/), where I worked on evolutionary algorithms and distributed training infrastructure respectively.
